Rising Above The Water

Flood Refief Donation Drive

In the guidance of the traditional values of helping the needy, NCC unit along with NSS, IIT Roorkee came together to contribute to the flood affected society in collaboration with the IX Battalion The Jat Regiment. The donation, collected from the residents of IIT Roorkee, was distributed at The Bengal Engineers Group Center, Roorkee. The grace of the occassion was enhanced by the presence of the Commandant of BEG Center himself. Other than this food stalls were also setup for the locals' convenience. The Flood Relief Donation Drive stands as a testament to the NCC's unwavering commitment to humanitarian causes, reminding us all that even in the midst of a crisis, compassion and collaboration can make a profound difference.

Unleashing Strength Within

Self-Defense Workshop

"The purpose of self-defence is not to win a fight, but to prevent one." Having this thought in mind, NCC IIT Roorkee in collaboration with the Taekwondo Club IIT Roorkee has been conducting workshops that blend the ancient martial art of Taekwondo with modern self-defense techniques. This initiative not only serves to enhance physical fitness but also empowers participants with the confidence and ability to respond effectively in challenging situations. Skilled instructors provides practical knowledge on techniques to defend yourself against potential threats. The workshop cover various aspects of self-defence, including basic strikes, kicks, blocks, and situational awareness.

Charting a Noble Path

Career Guidance Session on Joining the Armed Forces

A career in the armed forces is not only a matter of great prestige but also one of the most sought-afters in the world. Hear your own seniors speak about how to build one in the forces. In a bid to inspire the next generation of leaders and defenders of the nation, NCC IIT Roorkee, in collaboration with Career Development Cell, IIT Roorkee organizes an insightful Career Guidance Session on pursuing a career in the Armed Forces. This initiative aims to provide aspiring youth with a comprehensive understanding of the diverse opportunities, challenges, and rewards associated with a life in military service. NCC officers and Indian Army officers, share their experiences and insights, offering invaluable advice to those considering a path in the armed forces. The session covers various aspects such as eligibility criteria, selection processes, and the range of roles available across the Army, Navy, and Air Force. Through this initiative, the NCC not only contributes to building awareness about career options but also nurtures a sense of patriotism and duty among the participants. As cadets and young enthusiasts engage with professionals from the armed forces, they gain a profound appreciation for the significance of serving the nation and become better equipped to make informed decisions about their career paths. The Career Guidance Session stands as a testament to the NCC's commitment to shaping not just disciplined individuals but also future leaders dedicated to the noble cause of safeguarding the country.

March Together

Marathon for Unity and Endurance

In commemoration of National Youth Day, the National Cadet Corps (NCC) is set to host an invigorating Running Marathon, embodying the spirit of unity, endurance, and youthful enthusiasm. This event aligns with the ethos of National Youth Day, celebrating the energy and potential of the youth in contributing positively to society. NCC cadets, adorned in their distinctive uniforms, will lead the charge, exemplifying the discipline and commitment expected of the nation's young leaders. The marathon not only promotes physical fitness but also encapsulates the essence of youth engagement, fostering a sense of camaraderie and shared purpose. Participants from diverse backgrounds will converge, running together towards a common goal, symbolizing the unity and strength inherent in the nation's youth. As we mark National Youth Day, the NCC's Running Marathon stands as a testament to the organization's dedication to nurturing holistic development, encouraging resilience, and inspiring the youth to stride forward with purpose and unity.
