The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee is an esteemed institution that plays a pivotal role in nurturing the holistic development of its students. NCC at IIT Roorkee is known for instilling values of discipline, leadership, and patriotism among its cadets. The program provides a platform for students to engage in various activities such as drills, adventure sports, and social service, fostering a sense of responsibility and camaraderie. The NCC unit at IIT Roorkee not only contributes to the physical fitness and character building of its cadets but also serves as a vital link between the academic environment and the military ethos, creating well-rounded individuals who are not only academically adept but also socially aware and physically fit. The dedication and commitment of the NCC unit at IIT Roorkee contribute significantly to the overall development of future leaders and citizens of the nation.
